## Everything you need to write

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Manuscript Editor" icon="pencil">
    Write in Markdown with a distraction-free editor. Every save creates a new revision — your full history is always one click away.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Story Bible" icon="open-book">
    Keep your world consistent. Manage characters, locations, factions, items, lore, timelines, and research notes all linked to your manuscript.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reader Comments" icon="comment">
    Readers can annotate exact passages. Comments are anchored to character offsets and survive future revisions with fuzzy re-matching.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Revision Timeline" icon="list-format">
    Browse the full history of any chapter or book with side-by-side diffs. See exactly what changed and when.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Integration via MCP" icon="laptop">
    Connect AI assistants like Claude directly to your manuscripts using the built-in [MCP server](/guides/mcp-server). Create chapters, query your story bible, and more — without leaving your AI tool.
  </Card>
  <Card title="OPDS Feed" icon="download">
    Read your library on any OPDS-compatible app — KOReader, Calibre, Panels, and more. Your published and private books, always in sync. [Learn how to connect](/guides/opds-server).
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
